Enter the World of Competitive Pokémon

Overview

Pokémon Video Game Competition (VGC) is the official competitive format of the Pokémon franchise, bringing together players from around the world to showcase their strategies, creativity, and game knowledge in fast-paced, intense double-battle matches. This is your one-stop destination for jumping into Competitive Pokémon, whether you're brand-new or looking to level up your skills. Here, you'll learn the fundamentals of competitive play: smart teambuilding, efficient training, and intentional Pokémon breeding, which will enhance your Pokémon experience and guide you into the competitive scene. No experience is needed-just curiosity and a genuine love for Pokémon. With the right foundation, anyone can step onto the VGC stage and play with confidence.

Breeding

Create Pokémon for competitive play. This section explains how to intentionally breed Pokémon optimized for high-level battles. Breeding gives trainers full control over a Pokémon's IVs, nature, egg moves, and ability, ensuring your Pokémon starts with the strongest possible foundation before it is ever trained.
