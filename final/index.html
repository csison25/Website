Enter the World of Competitive Pokémon

Overview

Pokémon Video Game Competition (VGC) is the official competitive format of the Pokémon franchise, bringing players together from around the world to test their strategy, creativity, and game knowledge in intense double-battle matchups. This is your one-stop destination for jumping into Competitive Pokemon, whether you're brand-new or looking to level up your skills. Here you will find teambuilding, training, and Pokémon breeding concepts that will help elevate your Pokémon experience and guide you into the competitive scene. No experience needed-just curiosity and the love for Pokémon.
