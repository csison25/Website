Pokemon VGC

What is VGC?

Pokemon VGC is the official competitive format for the mainline Pokemon games. Players compete in live tournaments, regional events, online competitions, and the annual World Championships. These events are run by the Pokemon Company International and feature a standardized format and yearly rulesets. VGC focuses on fast-paced, strategic double battles. The format emphasizes predictions, synergy, and teamwork between Pokemon. Every season has a specific rulset that determines which Pokemon, items, or mechanics are allowed. The rules update regularly, keeping VGC fresh, balanced, and accessible to new players.

Why Doubles?

If you've played through the game, you would notice that majority of the battling is 1 vs 1. For VGC, a doubles format is used because it creates deeper and more interesting strategies.

Benefits of doubles

  • Greater team synergy: Giving certain moves and situations strategic importance
  • More choices: Each turn involves deciding two actions instead of one, increasing strategic and prediction depth.
  • Balanced gameplay: Doubles reduces the dominance of overly strong single Pokemon interactions
  • Reduces the likelihood of stall-focused matches (faster games)

General Rules

Every VGC season has a ruleset that outlines how battles work. These genreal rules stay consistent from year to year:

Battle Structure

  • Format: Double battles (2v2 on the field)
  • Bring 6 Pokemon, choose 4 for each match
  • Best-of-three in most official events
  • Time limit for turns and matches

Team Restrictions

  • Species Clause: No duplicate Pokemon
  • Item Clause: No duplicate items
  • Certain legendary and mythical Pokemon may be restricted or banned
  • No illegal moves or hacked Pokemon allowed

Game Mechanics

  • Battles use the current main series game
  • All Pokemon auto-set to level 50
  • Certain mechanics may be disabled depending on the season
    • Certain forms or moves in-game

Regulations

Regulations are seasonal rules that define which Pokemon, items, or mechanics are allowed. Each regulation period is denoted by a letter (A, B, C, etc.). They ensure balance and variety throughout the year, keeping tournaments fresh and preventing a single strategy from dominating for too long.

General Regulation Features

  • Which Pokemon are permitted or restricted
  • What new Pokemon from DLC or updates are added
  • Which legendary / Paradox / Mythical Pokemon are allowed or restricted
